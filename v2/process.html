The problem: too many items!

As the Zingtree product was used by larger teams, the number of decision trees in the account grew larger. This list had no mechanism to organize it.

  • Considerations

    Is research needed to get closer to the problem? What is the source of the request or idea? Internal stakeholders or customers?

  • Risks

    Are we addressing the right problem? Sometimes it may be a symptom of a different issue. The “5 whys” is a good method to explore cause and effect.
